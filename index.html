I'm Kate!

I'm a digital native with a penchant for finding the origins of online rumors and disinformation campaigns. I'm also interested in using data-oriented network analysis to identify, track, and combat online disinformation. Interested in extremism in “feminine” internet spaces like fandom, mom’s forums, and eating disorder communities.

I have written previously about influencer to extremism pipelines, decaying trust in internet personalities, and the role of video games in military recruitment. My work has been featured in Responsible Statecraft, Inkstick Media, Sunstroke, and more.

I also make interactive data visualizations for serious (nuclear arsenals) and fun (mapping the population of Pokemon regions) issues.
